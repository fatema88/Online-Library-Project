Book Name: Artificial Intelligence

Author: Stuart Russell

Category: Technology

Description:
This book introduces artificial intelligence in a clear and simple way
helping readers understand the basic concepts behind AI and how it works
It explains how machines can learn from data, make decisions, and solve problems that usually require human intelligence.
The book also presents real-life examples and applications of AI in areas such as technology, healthcare, and daily life, making it easier for beginners to connect theory with practice
Overall it is a great starting point for anyone who wants to explore the field of artificial intelligence and build a strong foundational understanding of how modern intelligent systems operate.
